Discover Artisan Delights at AABD Bakery

Located in the heart of Singapore CBD, AABD Bakery brings you the finest artisan sourdough, pastries, and seasonal cakes handcrafted with passion and quality ingredients. For working professionals who cherish quality and flavor, our bakery offers a delicious escape to brighten your day.

Our Menu

Handcrafted Sourdough

Our signature sourdough is slow-fermented for a deep, tangy flavor with a crusty golden exterior and soft crumb. Perfect for sandwiches or tasting on its own.

Delicious Pastries

Freshly baked every day, our pastries boast flaky layers, buttery richness, and creative flavors like almond croissants and chocolate twists–a sweet mid-day indulgence.

Seasonal Cakes

Celebrate with our limited-edition seasonal cakes, crafted with fresh ingredients and artistic flair. From fruity delights to indulgent chocolate, each cake is a masterpiece.

About Us

Founded by passionate bakers with deep roots in artisan traditions, AABD Bakery is committed to delivering quality and authenticity in every bite. We carefully select the finest ingredients, embrace slow fermentation, and blend craftsmanship and creativity to bring you exceptional breads, pastries, and cakes.

Our values center on sustainability, supporting local farmers, and fostering community connections in Singapore’s CBD. Enjoy a unique experience that elevates your daily routine with wholesome, delicious baked goods made the right way.
